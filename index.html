Memberships!

Associate Tier - $25/mo

  • the right to have your name on the list of members
  • access to the lab and shared equipment during the published hours of operation
  • and discounts with local manufacturers and suppliers

Basic Tier - $50/mo

  • associate tier benefits PLUS...
  • personal project box
  • the ability to vote in board elections
  • 24/7 access to the lab (when approved by community vote)

Plus Tier - $100/mo

  • basic tier benefits PLUS...
  • personal locker in place of the project box

Donations!

Want to send us money? Help us create more amazing things? HeatSync Labs is a 501(c)3 non-profit. Your help enables us to fulfill our educational mission, enhance our space, and any help is greatly appreciated!

If you need a 501(c)3 receipt for tax purposes, please say so in the notes field or contact info@heatsynclabs.org

Recurring Donations!

Sending money once is cool, but what's even better? Sending money every month! HeatSync values your sponsorship! Sponsorships are monthly recurring payments that support HeatSync Labs directly but confer no membership rights by themselves. Contact us if you would like promotion or recognition in return for your generous contribution.
